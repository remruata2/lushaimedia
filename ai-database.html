AI-Powered Data Management

Revolutionizing knowledge management with cutting-edge AI technology

Transform dormant scanned documents into intelligent, searchable databases with conversational AI capabilities.

Concept

The intervention of AI technology has globally revolutionized knowledge management, introducing unprecedented possibilities for data processing and information dissemination. This shift underscores the increasing necessity of leveraging IT for improving service delivery and data management across all economic sectors.

Challenges

The Land Revenue & Settlement (LR&S) Department in Aizawl faces significant operational inefficiencies in managing critical documentation, specifically Land Settlement Certificate (LSC) files and relevant records for Court Case & Dispute Settlement.

Data Inertia

The vast majority of these highly important documents exist solely as scanned copies, which renders the data dormant and inaccessible for immediate analysis.

Manual Constraint

Current processes are severely hampered by the need for manual summarization and information extraction, requiring immense effort and time, and preventing the valuable data from serving as a key source of information.

Solution: Lushai Media's AI Database

Lushai Media's AI Database provides secure Online Portal equipped with AI-Powered assistance to modernize the LR&S Department's data ecosystem. The system is meticulously designed to align with the Department's existing infrastructure and information patterns.

Intelligent Data Ingestion

The AI Database provides a robust data uploading feature to fetch and imports readable data from existing scanned documents thereby integrating OCR technology within the system.

Role-Based Access

Secure Admin login with subsequent role-based user management ensuring data security and proper access control.

AI Query Engine

All portal inputs are processed in the backend to enable AI capabilities, permitting privileged users to generate intelligent queries and statements.

Advanced Capabilities

All uploaded data are indexed and processed by the system, allowing users to instantly pull up any case with all relevant, cross-referenced details – achieving AI-like knowledge retrieval for revenue records specifically. The implementation deploys cutting-edge AI Technology that facilitates immediate and seamless data searching, fetching, summarization, analysis, and comparison – functionality that mirrors the user experience of advanced conversational AI platforms.
