Leikapui Studios

Creator-First OTT Platform

Premium streaming platform for Mizo cinema with flexible Pay Per View rentals and enterprise-grade content protection.

Concept

Leikapui Studios is a creator-first OTT platform specializing in Mizo cinema, where we produce original films in-house and curate select titles from independent filmmakers on a Pay Per View (PPV) rental model. Unlike subscription-heavy global platforms, users rent individual movies for on-demand access—typically with a 48-hour viewing window—making authentic Mizo storytelling affordable and flexible.

By controlling production and distribution, we ensure high-quality content that reflects Mizo culture, language, and values. For external filmmakers, we offer an industry-leading revenue share, empowering local talent to monetize their work directly while growing the platform's library with diverse, community-backed films.

Challenges

Before OTT platforms emerged, Mizo filmmakers faced significant logistical and financial burdens that limited their reach and revenue potential.

Logistical Barriers

Filmmakers had to physically travel across the state with projection equipment to screen films in local halls, consuming manpower, fuel, and time while reaching only limited audiences per showing.

Content Security Issues

Previous apps in Mizoram suffered from widespread piracy, with films leaked online or accounts shared among multiple users, causing filmmakers to lose substantial revenue and discouraging investment in quality productions.

Solution

Leikapui Studios tackles these challenges head-on with accessibility and ironclad protection.

Global Reach

By shifting distribution fully online, filmmakers no longer need to tour the state; a single upload reaches viewers statewide and globally via PPV rentals, drastically cutting costs and expanding reach.

Enterprise-Grade DRM

We implement enterprise-grade DRM encryption and limit each account to five registered devices, ensuring content security and preventing unauthorized sharing.

Forensic Watermarking

Dynamic forensic watermarking traces any illegal copy back to the source, providing an additional layer of protection for content creators.

Empowering Creators

These measures—combined with user education on supporting creators—ensure filmmakers retain maximum earnings while building trust in digital distribution as a viable, secure alternative to traditional screenings. With Leikapui Studios, viewers can now rent and watch movies instantly at affordable prices from home, eliminating barriers and creating new opportunities for Mizo cinema.

Platform Features

Multi-Device Experience

Native app development for iOS, Android, Smart TVs, and responsive web players ensuring seamless viewing across all devices.

Monetization Flexibility

Implement subscription (SVOD), transactional (TVOD), or ad-supported (AVOD) models to maximize revenue opportunities.
